Regulatory Compliance Overview (EU / US)

Cosmetic Grade

Suitable for use as raw materials in personal care and cosmetic formulations (topical applications), subject to formulator validation and local regulatory requirements.

  • COA & specifications
  • SDS (GHS format)
  • Allergen / IFRA information (where applicable)

Nutraceutical Grade

Suitable for dietary supplement manufacturing as a raw material. Final product compliance and claims remain the responsibility of the finished goods manufacturer/importer.

  • COA with assay/potency (where applicable)
  • MOA / test methods overview
  • Microbiology, heavy metals & contaminants data

Food Grade

Suitable for food and beverage applications as an ingredient/raw material for further processing. Compliance with local food regulations is determined by the importer/manufacturer.

  • COA + microbiology (as applicable)
  • Allergen statement (on request)
  • Storage, shelf life & traceability information

Regulatory disclaimer: All products supplied by Vaible Herbal are intended for use as raw materials for further manufacturing. Regulatory compliance and approval of the finished product is the responsibility of the finished goods manufacturer and/or importer.

Technical Knowledge

COA vs Specification Sheet

A specification sheet defines typical acceptance criteria and targets. A COA reports the actual batch results against those criteria. International buyers typically review both during supplier qualification.

Standardization & Marker Compounds

Standardized extracts are controlled to a defined marker compound or active profile to support consistency. Marker compounds and assay methods are specified on COA/MOA where applicable.
